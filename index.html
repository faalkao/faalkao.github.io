About Me

Hello! With a deep passion for AI & ML, Remote Sensing, and GIS, my journey began with a PhD in sustainable environmental studies, which laid the foundation for my expertise in areas like land use monitoring, carbon stock estimation, climate change adaptation, and AI-based flood susceptibility mapping. What drives me is the opportunity to make a real impact in addressing critical global challenges. I believe in the power of cutting-edge techniques to contribute to the greater good, and my work at CeADAR, University College Dublin's center for applied AI, has allowed me to do just that. Throughout my career, I've had the privilege to work on diverse projects, but one that holds a special place in my heart is the AIMVIE study. This project focuses on harnessing AI solutions to monitor mangrove forests and their vital role in carbon sequestration in Vietnam, an essential step in climate change adaptation.
